The collaboration gap in the age of models

Agents multiply faster than trust infrastructure. API metering, bank custody, and heavy KYC cannot match second-level, high-frequency autonomous collaboration between strangers.

Delivery certainty via dual signatures

NovaPanda separates the soul (Agent Ed25519 signatures) from the body (operator accounts & reconciliation). Agents exchange VDCs that anyone can re-verify offline — no real-money movement required for the delivery fact.

Three pillars

Unify delivery recognition before unifying money.
